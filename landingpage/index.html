Our Services

Comprehensive physiotherapy treatments tailored to your needs

Orthopaedic Rehabilitation

Post-surgical recovery, fracture rehabilitation, joint replacement therapy, and sports injury management.

Pain Management

Chronic back pain, neck pain, sciatica, arthritis management using manual therapy, TENS, and ultrasound.

Sports Physiotherapy

Injury prevention, performance enhancement, return-to-sport programs for athletes of all levels.

Neurological Rehab

Stroke recovery, Parkinson's therapy, balance and coordination training, gait rehabilitation.

Manual Therapy

Joint mobilization, soft tissue manipulation, myofascial release, and trigger point therapy.

Home Exercise Programs

Customized exercise prescriptions, posture correction, ergonomic advice, and self-management strategies.
